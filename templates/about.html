Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Founded in 1950, The Donut Hole began as a small family-owned bakery in Manila with a passion for creating artisanal donuts that bring joy to every bite. What started as a humble storefront has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our innovative flavors and commitment to quality.

Our master bakers combine traditional techniques with creative flair, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local suppliers whenever possible. Each donut is handcrafted with care, ensuring every customer experiences the perfect balance of flavor and texture.
