Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have a selection of gluten-free and vegan donuts available daily. Please ask our staff for current options.

Can I place a large order for an event?

Absolutely! We cater for events large and small. Please contact us at least 72 hours in advance for orders over 50 donuts.

Do you deliver?

We offer delivery within Metro Manila through our website or via food delivery partners.

How long do your donuts stay fresh?

Our donuts are best enjoyed the same day, but will stay fresh for up to 2 days when stored properly in an airtight container.
